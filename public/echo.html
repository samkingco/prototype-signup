Patients at the heart of everything we do

Our co-founders both take medicine to manage long-term conditions, so Echo is first and foremost a patient-led service.

Run by qualified pharmacists. Backed by LloydsPharmacy

Your prescription will be checked by our qualified pharmacists and posted from our registered pharmacy in London. We process all your medicines by hand and check them meticulously, just like your local pharmacy.

And we’re part of the LloydsPharmacy family, so you can be sure your prescription is in safe hands.

Convenient for you. Good for the NHS

We cover the cost of postage, so ordering your medicines with us does not cost the NHS anything extra. We have a contract to provide NHS services, and we're recommended in the NHS apps library.

We’re also a proud member of the NHS Innovation Accelerator, a partnership between the NHS and England's leading universities.
